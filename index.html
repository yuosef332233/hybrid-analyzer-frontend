About Football Predictor AI

POWERED BY CLAUDE AI · EST. 2026

Football Predictor AI is a fully automated match prediction platform that uses real live football data combined with advanced AI analysis to generate win probabilities and match insights.

How It Works

Our system fetches live data from professional football APIs and feeds it into our AI engine powered by Claude AI (Anthropic). The AI analyzes multiple factors including recent form, attack and defense strength, home advantage, and historical performance to generate predictions.

What We Analyze

  • 📊 Last 5 match results and form trajectory
  • ⚽ Goals scored and conceded per game averages
  • 🏟️ Home ground advantage factor
  • 🔥 Winning and losing streaks
  • 🛡️ Defensive and attacking strength ratings
  • 📈 Team momentum and confidence index

Competitions Covered

We cover all major European competitions: Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1, UEFA Champions League, Europa League, Conference League, FA Cup, Copa del Rey, Coppa Italia, DFB Pokal, Coupe de France, and more.

Powered by Claude AI

Our prediction engine is built on top of Claude AI by Anthropic — one of the world's most advanced AI systems. This allows us to process complex statistical patterns and generate accurate, data-driven football insights automatically.
